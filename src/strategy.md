# Solution Strategy

We will have partial sums of the form:

\\[ S(n)=\frac{1}{v\_1}+\frac{1}{v\_2}+\frac{1}{v\_3}+\\dots+\frac{1}{v\_n} \\]

Choose a *p* so that 

\\[ S(n) = \frac{a}{b\times p\^e} \quad \text{where}\quad (a,p)=1\quad\text{and}\quad(b,p)=1\\]

## Math Induction

\\[S(1),S(2),\dots\\]

\\[p\mid S(v\_0)\\]
